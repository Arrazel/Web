Introduction

In the modern world, solving environmental problems through technology has become an integral part of innovation. One of the ways to achieve environmental safety and tracking is through satellites that perform various monitoring tasks.

Our goal

Creating an innovative solution using advanced technologies. The main objective of such solutions is to reduce the time needed to transmit information from a satellite, integrate AI for maximum accuracy and efficiency of forecasts, and collect information both on Earth and in space.

The satellite's task

The satellite's mission is to protect the environment and human safety by analyzing fires and transmitting them as soon as possible for an immediate response from those responsible. The system must notify about a fire in a short time, analyze and make a forecast about the area, probability, and add up all the data to provide them as a matter of urgency.

What do we offer?

We offer a comprehensive approach to solving this problem using AI in the task of data analysis, as well as using a satellite channel to establish communication with those responsible in the B2G and B2B concepts, we also use technologies that allow us to transfer information as quickly as possible for the fastest possible response to fires!
